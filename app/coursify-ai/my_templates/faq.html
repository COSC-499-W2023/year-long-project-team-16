FAQs

On the homepage, you'll find a navigation bar with options: HOME, MY CONTENT, SETTINGS, and AI ASSISTANT. There's also a LOGOUT button for signing out.

Go to the "Generate Content" section on the homepage. Input the desired Length of Content in words, select the Difficulty level (from 0 to 3), and enter the Main Topics for your content. Optionally, upload a PDF file for additional material or reference, then click "Generate."

To update your account details, click on the "SETTINGS" tab. Here, you can update your First Name, Last Name, Password, and Email. You can also upload or change your profile picture under "Profile Picture."

For assistance, click on "AI ASSISTANT" in the navigation bar. A chat window will open for you to type your message and receive a response from the AI support system.

Yes, you can upload a PDF file by clicking "Choose File" in the Generate Content section to provide additional material or reference for your content generation.

Click on "MY CONTENT" in the navigation bar to view, review, or download all the content and notes you have created or uploaded.
